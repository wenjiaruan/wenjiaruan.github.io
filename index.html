Bio
I have graduated with a PhD in summer 2015, from Computer Science and Engineering Department, Lehigh University, supervised by Professor Michael Spear. I received my B.S in Electronic Information Science and Technology from Ocean University of China. Before I came to the U.S, I lived in a beautiful coastal city Qingdao, China.

In Aug. 2015, I joined Qualcomm Research Silicon Valley (QRSV) . The project I've been working on is a power-aware heterogeneous parallel programming suite called Qualcomm Symphony (formerly known as Qualcomm MARE).
